## Content

### Overview (English)

Fluent is a free, open-source alternative to Wispr Flow for macOS. Get the same powerful voice-to-text experience without the subscription — just bring your own OpenAI API key.

Press a global hotkey, speak naturally, and your words are automatically transcribed and pasted directly into any application. No recurring fees, no locked features, fully transparent code.

**Why Fluent over Wispr Flow?**
- **100% Free** — No subscription, no premium tiers, no hidden costs
- **Open Source** — Fully transparent, audit the code yourself
- **Your API Key** — Pay only for what you use directly to OpenAI
- **Privacy-First** — All data stays local, zero tracking or telemetry

**All the features you expect:**
- **Global Hotkeys** — Fn or Option+Space to record from anywhere
- **AI Transcription** — Powered by OpenAI Whisper for accurate speech-to-text
- **Smart Enhancement** — Optional GPT-4 mini for punctuation and grammar
- **Auto-Paste** — Text appears instantly at your cursor
- **Recording History** — Searchable history with timestamps
- **Multi-Language** — 12+ languages including Arabic, with auto-detection
- **Menu Bar App** — Runs quietly in the background

Requires macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later.
